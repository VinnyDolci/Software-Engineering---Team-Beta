This project is an adaptation of everyone's favorite game to lose money at, Left-Right-Center! We have taken the core components of LRC and added some new dice outcomes such as: Link: You and another player's fates are tied together. Until another player rolls this die. Give to all: You have to pay out 1 BetaBuck to every player in the game Golden Roll: You immediately get all of the BetaBucks that are in the pot And More! This project is currently under devlopment and full functionality will not be available until the end of the Spring semester 2022.
